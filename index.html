Ecological Communities Organization is a CA non-profit corporation and federal 501(c)(3) with a vision:

Cities more like gardens, farms, orchards, forests, meadows and wild natural places.

mission:

To radically and safely transform human habitats and lifestyles around principles of permaculture

projects:

Night Market: free food rescue and distribution network
Circle of Bees: suburban beekeeping project

synopsis:

eco is an art project created on the idea that the great challenge of our times, to radically transform civilization around princples of permaculture, must begin with aesthetics, fiction, fantasy and the assorted schemes and crafts of sensory and imaginative art.
